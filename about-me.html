About Me

Get to know a bit about who I am and what drives me.

Hi, I'm a junior data science enthusiast with a growing portfolio of practical projects in healthcare, e-commerce, and social media analytics. I enjoy transforming raw data into meaningful insights using tools like Python, SQL, and Scikit-learn. Whether it's building machine learning models, analyzing customer behavior, or extracting sentiment from YouTube comments, I’m passionate about applying data to solve real-world problems.

As I continue to grow, I intend to pursue further certifications and training to deepen my skills and expand my understanding of advanced machine learning and data engineering tools.

I'm also excited to impart the knowledge I’ve acquired — whether through collaboration, mentorship, or content creation — and I welcome every opportunity to both learn and contribute.
